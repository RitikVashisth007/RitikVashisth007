## 🚀 ChirpBack — [chirpback.io](https://chirpback.io)

A production feedback, surveys & announcements platform with AI in the loop. Highlights I've worked on:

- **Multi-source feedback inbox** with auto-tagged sentiment, category & topic (in-app widget, email, App Store / Google Play reviews, CSV, API)
- **Surveys engine** — 30+ question types, branching logic, NPS / CSAT / CES
- **Two-way backlog sync** with Jira, Linear, Asana & Trello
- **Close-the-loop notifications** — automatically notify the people who asked when their request ships
- **14 live integrations** (Slack, HubSpot, Salesforce, Zendesk, Intercom, Zapier, Make, and more)

> Architecture spans a multi-tenant backend, real-time sync workers, an integrations layer, and a component-driven Next.js frontend.
